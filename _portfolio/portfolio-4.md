**[ConversationAlign](https://github.com/Reilly-ConceptsCognitionLab/ConversationAlign)** is an R package for organizing and analyzing linguistic alignment on various dimensions between speakers in dyadic conversations.

Reilly J, Ulichney V, Sacks B (2025). ConversationAlign: Process Text and Compute Linguistic Alignment in Conversation Transcripts. R package version 0.4.0, https://github.com/Reilly-ConceptsCognitionLab/ConversationAlign
